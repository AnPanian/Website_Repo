In the personal website project I was able to explore my own design tools. Instead of taking a plan that was given to me I had to generate my own. About Me

I am originally from Portland, Or. I believe that's where I get my edgier design ideas from. Portland is full of diverse cultures and amazing street art. After my husband joined the military, we had the oppertunity to travel throughout the west part of the U.S. We've had the privilege to live in Tucson, Az, Pocatello, Id, and are now settling into Colorado Springs, Co. When I'm not gaming or reading indoors on cold wintery days, we enjoy hiking, and exploring our new homes towns.
